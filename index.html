Keys to Success

We are a premiere nationally recognized certification program designed specifically for the debt management and debt settlement industries. Curriculum is based on a formal job/needs analysis conducted by ACE affiliate Center for Financial Literacy and Florida Institute of Technology Center for Professional Development.

Mobile-ready course units...

Explore essential counseling skills, consumer protection legislation and current issues surrounding credit and debt. Each of five modules includes links to keywords, printable activities and image-rich slide presentations.

Predict your success

Predict your success by taking the self-scoring Practice Exam. A passing score is 70% or higher. When you are ready to test, submit the REQUEST EXAM FORM. Contact ACE support if you need assistance with any aspect of the testing process.
