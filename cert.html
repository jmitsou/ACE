STEPS TO CERTIFICATION


1 ) SIGN UP FOR ONLINE COURSE

Sign up and gain access to the online exam study course which includes five interactive modules, five review slide presentations and a 100-item practice exam.

2 ) REQUEST THE EXAM

When you have completed the study course and scored high on the practice exam, choose a testing site (community college or private learning center) convenient to your location. Submit the REQUEST EXAM FORM with payment and receive your Exam Ticket by email. You may then schedule the exam with the proctor indicated in the form.

3 ) PASS THE EXAM

On the day of the exam, present your Exam Ticket Number and Photo ID to the proctor. Typically, there will be a nominal fee for proctoring at the testing site. You will have up to two hours to complete the 100­item multiple­choice exam. Score 70% or higher to pass.
