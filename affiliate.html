Center for Financial Literacy (CFL) CFL is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit agency providing research and development in consumer financial education. CFL conducted the first formal job/needs analysis of the debt management and debt settlement industries in conjunction with Florida Tech’s Center for Professional Development. Keys to Success curriculum is based, in part, on this important research.

Association of Debt Counselors (ADC) While ACE delivers educational resources, ADC Advisory Board sets and monitors curriculum and testing standards and awards designations to successful certification candidates. Certification includes membership with ADC.
