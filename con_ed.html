CEU Process

Please read the following information regarding CEU requirements and reporting procedures. Our goal is to maintain the highest standards while providing web-based processes for obtaining, completing and reporting CEUs.

1. Register for ACE continuing education. Select from our list of current offerings. Independent study courses are available in ebook format.

2. Submit CEU completion form. This form includes information about the course, number of CEUs, date of completion and an informal assessment. Once submitted, you will be able to create and print your Certificate of Completion.

3. Check your MyACE dashboard. ACE updates your file so you can view your continuing education status. When 16 CEUs are completed, you can apply for certification renewal.

GUIDELINES FOR CONTINUING EDUCATION

If you have a course you would like to submit for approval, please refer to the guidelines listed below. Then, fill out the CEU Approval Form (Click here).

1. Definition of CEU

Each unit of continuing education consists of 1 hour of attendance or application. A program of study worth 4 CEUs should take 4 hours to complete.

2. Determining hours

Continuing education units for personally attended seminars or classes equal their duration. An 8 hour seminar is worth 8 CEUs.

3. Acceptable topics

All subjects that relate to personal financial management as well as issues surrounding credit and debt are acceptable. Eligibility is not restricted to strictly technical issues, but also includes how counselors deliver their services. This would include communication skills, counseling skills, the business use of computers, as well as topics concerning professional ethics. Ask yourself, “Does this subject assist me in serving my clients?” If the answer to the question is “Yes”, then the topic would be included.

4. Program formats

CEUs can be earned through seminar or class participation or through self-study. Self-study materials can take the form of books, films, audio tapes, CDs, and online learning.

5. Submission requirements

Submit continuing education by filling out the CEU Approval Form (click here).

6. Time frame

Upon receipt of a CEU for approval, ace will either make recommendations or grant approval for CEU credits within 14 days.


Continuing Education Offerings

In order to effectively counsel clients in debt, it is necessary to increase, update and maintain professional skills and knowledge through approved continuing education. Our programs are recognized and approved by the Certified Financial Planner (CFP; #3005), The State of South Carolina (#0618100) and NeighborWorks® America. In addition, our curriculum is taught at universities for college credit i.e. Western Wisconsin College.

Bankruptcy & Pre-Bankruptcy Counseling 8 CEUS

This self-study course examines Chapter 7 and Chapter 13 Bankruptcies in light of new legislation and consumer trends. Mandatory pre-bankruptcy counseling and debtor education are also explored. Students can choose to receive a Certificate of Completion or upgrade to Pre-Bankruptcy Specialist designation by taking the 50-item standardized exam. $75

Credit Reports & Credit Scoring 8 CEUs

The credit game is easier to play once you know the score. Course content includes a detailed explanation of credit scoring, strategies for maintaining a good score and credit misinformation. Earn CEUs and a Certificate of Completion or opt to take the 50-item standardized test and receive the designation, Credit Scoring Specialist. $75

The Big Three: Housing, Education and Retirement 8 CEUs

Top financial goals, generally speaking, are homeownership, pursuing higher education and preparing for retirement. This self-study course considers topics and issues about these important measures of financial success. $75

Understanding Personality Types 8 CEUs

Find out about one of the nost widely used personality assessment in the world: the Myers-Briggs Personality Type Indicator. Understanding the personalities around can be – to say the least – highly beneficial. $75

Optimum Customer Service 4 CEUs

Identify your customers! Who are they? They are not only clients but co-workers …and you. Easy reading focuses in on a key discover: providing extraordinary customer service can be exhilarating! $38

The Power Of Negotiation 4 CEUs

This self-study course is designed with the debt negotiator in mind. The nature of conflict and conflict resolution are explored through text and self-assessment. $38
